Srinivas University is a private university located in Mangalore, Karnataka. The University was established in 2015 by the A. Shama Rao Foundation through the Srinivas University Act, 2013. It is a UGC-recognised University and the core lies is in its faculty resource, which is a balanced blend of rich expertise from Academia, Industry Consultancy & Research. It is part of the Srinivas Group of Institutions. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes in various streams such as Medicine and Health Sciences, IT and Software, Business & Management Studies, Engineering, Accounting & Commerce, Design, Hospitality and Travel, Nursing, Aviation, Banking Finance and Insurance, etc Click on the below link to know more about Srinvas University.
